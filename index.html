Parry Pools | Respected, Trained, Quality Residential and Commercial Swimming Pool Repair and New Construction!

For over 35 years Parry Pools, located in Jacksonville, Florida, has been a leader in residential and commercial swimming pool new construction and swimming pool repair and maintenance. With our cutting edge technology and advanced design programs, we are able to turn any greenspace into a tropical paradise.

Our services are not limited to new construction. Our team can transform your old and outdated pool, equipment and deck into a more energy efficient and modern version of the pool you originally fell in love with.

If keeping your pool clean and properly balanced has become more than you can handle, our pool maintenance team can keep your pool water balanced and operating at the safest levels.

Company Profile

Parry Pools was founded in 1975 by William E. Parry, Jr. With the help of his family, Mr. Parry built Parry Pools into the area's most well respected service company. In 1989, William Parry, III, joined forces with his dad, and started the commercial and residential construction division. For the past 35+ years, Parry's reputation has remained strong and has expanded throughout Florida and the surrounding areas.
